This Preschool Life is our attempt to bring you the ideas, curiousity, and joy that come from young people sharing their perspectives. We're made in the back of a children's center by folks who are under the age of five. Young people use the "podcasting station" to discuss whatever is on their minds.

We support young people in translating their passions into creative projects they care about — a lifelong skill that helps them take their interests seriously and do meaningful, creative work.

While schools are on hiatus, we'll offer ideas for activities and prompts for conversations. We welcome episode and blog ideas!

If you and your children would like to contribute to our podcast or blog, please email us at team@thispreschoollife.org.
